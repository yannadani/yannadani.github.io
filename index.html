About Me

I'm currently doing my final year undergraduate at NITK Surathkal, with a major in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. I am interested in doing research in Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence. I have previously worked with Prof Soma Biswas at Electrical Department, IISc, Bangalore on Action Recognition from Skeletal Joints of Kinect Sensor. I interned at INRIA, France under Francois Bremond and worked on large scale activity recognition from RGBD data using Deep Learning. I am currently doing my bachelor's thesis under the supervision of Prof Soma Biswas, on motion preserving image representation of a video for video analysis tasks like Action recognition. Apart from research in core computer vision and AI, I am also interested in applying Machine Learning and Computer Vision techniques to 'out-of-the-box' problems like Selfie Detection and Personality based Scenario Response Prediction. In my free time, I like to read novels - especially thrillers like The day of the Jackal and Killing Floor, and write chunks of prose and poetry, some of which can be found in my blog. In addition, I like to travel a lot, and hope to discover the marvels across all parts of the globe.
