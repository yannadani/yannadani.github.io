Bio

I am a PhD student in Causal Inference and Machine Learning at MPI for Intelligent Systemsa>, Germany and KTH Stockholm, Sweden as part of ELLIS PhD program. I completed my master's degree in Electrical Engineeting and Information Technology at ETH Zürich. During my master's studies, I was a research intern at Mila under the supervision of Prof. Yoshua Bengio.

Before coming to ETH Zürich, I completed my Bachelor's degree from NITK Surathkal. During this time, I interned at INRIA under Francois Bremond and at IACV, Indian Institute of Science under Soma Biswas working on action recognition and zero-shot learning.
